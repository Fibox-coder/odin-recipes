Crusty Pizza

A tasty, quick pizza crust that uses no yeast.

A quick and easy recipe for a delicious home made pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup fat-free milk
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl
  2. stir in milk and olive oil until a soft dough forms
  3. Turn dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead 10 times.
  4. Shape dough into a ball.
  5. Cover dough with an inverted bowl and let sit for 10 minutes.
  6. Roll dough into a 12-inch circle on a baking sheet.
